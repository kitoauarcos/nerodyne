What you'll learn

From idea to automated signal

🔬

Research and hypotheses

Turn a market observation into a testable, rules-based hypothesis. How to think in factors such as value, quality, growth and momentum, and how to judge what actually carries an edge.

🧪

Honest backtesting

Build a point-in-time backtest with no look-ahead or survivorship bias. This is the difference between a genuine edge and a curve that only looks good in hindsight.

🛡️

Risk and hedging

Size positions, model drawdowns, and understand how option-based protection such as put ladders and earnings hedges reshapes a return distribution.

⚙️

Automation

Connect a model to data feeds and a scheduler so it re-runs itself and flags changes, with no manual spreadsheet work.

📲

Your own delivery

Route your own signals to Telegram or email, using the same delivery pattern NERODYNE runs, applied to your strategy.

🎓

Mentorship

Direct feedback on your model and your process from the Nerodyne team, focused on your ideas rather than ours.

Why the approach matters

A model you understand is a model you can hold through a drawdown.

A signal you cannot explain becomes worthless the moment it stops working, because you have no way to judge whether to trust it or abandon it. When you have built the strategy yourself, chosen the factors, run the backtest and sized the risk, you understand its character: where it performs, where it struggles, and when to stay disciplined.

That is the purpose of this program. It does not hand you a finished answer, and it does not expose Nerodyne's models. It gives you a repeatable method you can apply to your own ideas.
